# Business Model

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Target | Price | Limits |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Trial | $0 | 1 engram, 100 sources, 50 queries/mo, 3 integrations, no background agents |
| **Pro** | Power users | $20/mo | Unlimited engrams/sources/queries, all agents, all integrations, priority compilation |
| **Team** | Small teams | $15/user/mo | Shared engrams, roles, 10 seats, cross-engram queries, team integrations |
| **Enterprise** | Organizations | Custom | SSO, audit, compliance, custom LLM, SLA, managed integrations |

Usage-based overage for heavy compilation (>N agent-hours/month) to protect LLM cost margins.
